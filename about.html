About us

We are the leading Industry In Africa.

Empressors was found in 2000 by Mrs.Sangou who took the initiative to start the business after the bankruptcy of LAOS Entreprise and now the company makes approximately 10 billion dollars per year. It all started when Mrs.Sangou was experimenting with school owned solar panels. She had huge interest in solar panels and everyday, she would fidget with some equipments just to see how they are made. Just after school, she launched a small company: IntsNet, which aims to help potential small business owners by preparing and filing the documents necessary to start a business in any state. For those already in business, IntsNet can help with ongoing paperwork like filing annual reports, changing a company name, and more. Though she liked the idea of the company, she wanted to be involved in something related to what she loves.
Two years later she founded Lautexs in Ghana Africa, which is now Empressors And in a year, The company gained international recognition.
